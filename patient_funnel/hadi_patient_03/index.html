You need to tell HADI about:

Pain Levels

- Whenever you want, just text the number

- Yes, just a number from zero to ten (0-10)

- Then go on with your life

Medications

- When you take something, text HADI the name of your medication

- Using the keyword: med

- Like this: med Excedrin

Treatments

- When you receive a treatment, text HADI the name of your treatment

- Using the keyword: tx

- Like this: tx botox
