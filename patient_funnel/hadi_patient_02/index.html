"My words didn’t seem to carry much weight with my doctor"

So I created HADI

Instead of writing a fancy mobile app - I chose the app everyone already has

- I just text my data to HADI

- Yep. Simple text messaging!

- All the fancy is in the analytics and reporting.

You need to tell HADI about:

  • - Pain Levels
  • - Medications
  • - Treatments
  • - Notable Events

Pain Level

- Whenever you want, just text the number

- Yes, just a number from zero to ten (0-10)

- Then go on with your life

Medication

- When you take something, text HADI the name of your medication

- Using the keyword: med

- Like this: med Excedrin

Treatment

- When you receive a treatment, text HADI the name of your treatment

- Using the keyword: tx

- Like this: tx botox

Note

- When something notable happens, text HADI a short comment

- Using the keyword: note

- Like this: note I didn’t sleep well again last night

HADI listens AND HADI gives back!!

To get reports from HADI, text HADI your email ID

- Using the keyword: email

- Like this: email jill@example.com

Then, you can ask HADI for a report using the keyword: rpt

- Like this: rpt (for all the reports)

- Or: rpt problem days (# problem days per week)

- Or: rpt history (entire log)

For instance, text: rpt history

to see your entire log history at a glance with all your medications and treatments

Finally, Take your HADI Reports to your Doctor

Remember, Information is Power!

With better information, your doctor will make better decisions

You will have confidence that you have been heard
